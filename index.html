Where is Tetouan ?

Tetouan

Tetouan is a city located in northern Morocco, situated on the Mediterranean coast and nestled at the base of the Rif Mountains. Known for its rich history and unique blend of cultures, Tetouan has earned the nickname "The White Dove" due to the whitewashed buildings in its historic Medina, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The city has a distinctive Andalusian influence, evident in its architecture, music, and cuisine, reflecting its history as a refuge for Andalusian Muslims and Jews fleeing Spain during the Reconquista.
